How It Works

Adding Overlays to the Map

Use the "Maps" dropdown menu at the top to select and add all of the overlays you want!

View & Re-order Overlays

The Overlays button at the top right allows you to toggle map overlays and re-order them so you can choose which overlay displays on top of the others.

The "Base Maps" section lets you choose between Google Roads and Google Satellite.

The "Overlays" section lets you toggle the display of the overlays you have picked from the Maps dropdown menu.

The "Selected Overlays Order" section lets you modify the order in which the overlays are displayed.

Search by Poll #

The binoculars button at the top left corner allows you to search the map by poll number.

If it says "location not found", just try again and the results should display the second time.
