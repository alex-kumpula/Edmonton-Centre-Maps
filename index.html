How It Works

Search by Poll #

The binoculars button at the top left corner allows you to search the map by poll number.

If it says "location not found", just try again and the results should display the second time.

View Legend & Layers

The legend/layers button at the top right allows you to toggle map layers and view their corresponding legends to make sense of the data.
